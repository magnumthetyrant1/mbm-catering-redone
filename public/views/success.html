"As entrepreneurs in this tough economy, you don't ask 'Is this a good time?' you just do," Mel says. WOW factor:

Mel and her specially selected teams treat guests with respect and always show happy smiley faces. Her experience with International groups touring Cape Town, weddings and corporate events as well as mass catering for camps gives her company the edge as she understands both FIVE Star as well as canteen catering and guides her teams accordingly. Working within the economic downturn, Mel was able to maintain her volume by adjusting prices to suit any pocket.
